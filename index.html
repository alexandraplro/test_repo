ABOUT ME

After more than a decade leading public affairs and media strategy across government, nonprofit, and multilateral sectors, I pivoted toward tech to deepen my problem-solving toolkit. I now specialize in C# development, code refactoring, and modular architecture, with a growing interest in AI ethics and digital governance.

My approach to development is grounded in clarity, iteration, and empathy — whether I'm optimizing a console-based task manager or collaborating on cross-functional teams. I thrive on feedback, love debugging, and believe that good code (like good storytelling) should be honest, elegant, and purposeful.

When I'm not coding, I'm writing memoir-style reflections, advocating for civil rights, or exploring how technology can support global justice. I'm fluent in English, Spanish, and French, and always eager to learn something new — especially if it challenges me to think deeper.
